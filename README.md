## 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains the Digital Literacy Portfolio developed as part of the CSE0001 course. The project is based on the role of a *Student Digital Ambassador*, focusing on building essential digital skills required for academic and professional growth.

The portfolio covers five key areas: digital awareness, online presence, coding practice, professional communication, and cybercrime awareness. All tasks are supported with relevant screenshots and documentation.

## 📝 Tasks Summary

### 🔹 Task 1: Infographic  
Created a one-page infographic covering digital footprint, cybersecurity, and online etiquette to promote safe digital practices.

### 🔹 Task 2: Digital Portfolio  
Developed professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish a strong digital identity and showcase technical skills.

### 🔹 Task 3: Platforms  
Completed a beginner coding challenge on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to assess digital literacy awareness.

### 🔹 Task 4: Digital Etiquette  
Drafted professional emails and created a social media checklist highlighting responsible online communication.

### 🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness  
Researched UPI fraud, created a case study, and developed a prevention guide including cybercrime helpline **1930**.
